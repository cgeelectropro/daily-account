## Overview

Daily Account is a spiritual accountability app designed for the CMFI discipleship tradition. It helps you track daily spiritual disciplines and share weekly reports with your disciple maker.

**Your data stays on your device.** Daily Account does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

---

## Data Collection & Storage

### What we store

All data is stored **locally on your device only**, using SQLite and SharedPreferences:

- Daily discipline logs (Bible reading, prayer times, literature notes, evangelism contacts, etc.)
- Weekly report archives
- App settings (language preference, disciple maker name/contact, reminder times)
- Biometric/PIN lock preference (the biometric data itself is managed by your device OS, not by us)
- Timer sessions (prayer, Bible reading durations)
- Voice note recordings (stored as local files)
- Proclamation counts

### What we do NOT collect

- No personal identification information is sent to any server
- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising data
- No location data
- No browsing history
- No cookies or web tracking
- No cloud sync or remote storage

---

## Permissions

Daily Account requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Biometric / Fingerprint | Optional app lock for privacy |
| Notifications | Daily reminders and weekly report prompts |
| Exact Alarm | Reliable reminder scheduling |
| Microphone | Voice note recording (optional feature) |
| Internet | Sending reports via email/WhatsApp (user-initiated only) |
| Wake Lock | Ensuring scheduled reminders fire reliably |

**All permissions are used solely for the stated purposes.** No data from these permissions is transmitted externally.

---

## Data Sharing

Daily Account does **not** share your data with any third parties.

The only time data leaves your device is when **you** explicitly choose to:

- **Send a weekly report** via email, WhatsApp, or clipboard (you choose the recipient)
- **Export a backup file** (JSON format, saved to a location you choose)
- **Share a PDF report** (generated locally, shared via your device's share sheet)

In all cases, you initiate the action and choose the recipient.

---

## Data Backup & Deletion

- **Backup:** You can export all your data as a JSON file from Settings. This file stays wherever you save it.
- **Restore:** You can import a previously exported backup file.
- **Deletion:** Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently. There is no cloud copy.

---

## Children's Privacy

Daily Account does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal data from any user regardless of age.
