## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Daily Account is a beautifully crafted spiritual accountability app designed for disciples who want to grow faithfully in their walk with God.

Built for the CMFI discipleship tradition, Daily Account helps you track your daily spiritual disciplines — Bible reading, prayer, DDEG meditation, Christian literature, evangelism, fasting, giving, church life, and discipleship — then generates a structured weekly report to share with your disciple maker.

KEY FEATURES

- Daily Discipline Tracking: Log your Bible reading (chapters, references), prayer times, DDEG scripture meditation, Christian literature, evangelism contacts, fasting, giving, church attendance, and discipleship activities
- Smart Stopwatch: Built-in timer for prayer and Bible reading with automatic duration logging
- Proclamation Counter: Tap to count your daily proclamations of "Jesus Christ is the Lord"
- Weekly Reports: Auto-generated weekly summary with completion stats, streaks, and detailed logs
- Multiple Sharing Options: Send reports via email, WhatsApp, or clipboard
- PDF Export: Generate beautiful PDF reports for archiving
- Home Screen Widgets: Four purpose-built widgets — Scripture Card, Discipline Bar, Full Altar, and Proclamation Counter — for at-a-glance spirituality right from your home screen
- Backup & Restore: Export and import your data as JSON files
- Biometric Lock: Protect your journal with fingerprint or PIN
- Voice Notes: Record audio reflections alongside your written notes
- Bilingual Support: Full English and French interface
- Daily Reminders: Configurable notifications to keep you faithful
- Beautiful Design: Espresso and gold-leaf aesthetic with Cormorant Garamond typography
- 100% Offline: All data stays on your device. No account needed. No cloud. No tracking.

DESIGNED FOR DISCIPLES

Daily Account is purpose-built for the CMFI accountability tradition. The seven core disciplines (Bible, Literature, DDEG, Prayer, Evangelism, Fasting, Giving) plus Church and Discipleship tracking follow the proven structure that has built faithful disciples for decades.

YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE

We believe your spiritual journal is sacred and private. Daily Account stores everything locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to any server. You choose when and with whom to share your reports.
